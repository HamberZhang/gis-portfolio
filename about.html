About me

Hello! I'm Hongbo Zhang, a Master's student in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

I specialize in using spatial data to tell compelling stories. My technical expertise spans across GIS & Remote Sensing (ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Mapbox) and Programming (Python, HTML). I am passionate about transforming complex datasets into meaningful visual narratives that support sustainable planning and decision-making.

Prior to my graduate studies, I earned a B.S. in Supply Chain Management from Bellevue University. This background gives me a unique perspective on supply chain management and logistical efficiency, which I now apply to spatial analysis challenges.
